Why Website Speed Matters

User Experience

Fast-loading websites provide better user experience, with lower bounce rates and higher engagement. Users expect pages to load in 2 seconds or less.

SEO Impact

Site speed is a ranking factor for search engines. Faster websites tend to rank higher in search results, bringing more organic traffic.

Conversion Rates

Every second counts. A 1-second delay in page load time can result in a 7% reduction in conversions. Speed directly impacts your bottom line.
